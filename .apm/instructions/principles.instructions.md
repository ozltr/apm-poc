# Conventions

Guidance for operating as a single developer within this system.

## Principles
- **Scope ruthlessly** — build for current needs, not hypothetical scale.
- **Favor simplicity** — minimize moving parts and complexity.
- **Automate pain** — CI/CD, testing, and repetitive tasks early.
- **Design for visibility** — logs, metrics, and traceability.
- **Modular over distributed** — prefer clean structure over fragmentation.
- **Document decisions** — leave context for the future self.
- **Secure by default** — least privilege and safe handling of data.
- **Use proven tools** — reliability over novelty.

## Plan mode

- Always have an executive summary
- 1-5 bullet points
- Focus on key actions
- DO NOT EXCEED 300 words.
